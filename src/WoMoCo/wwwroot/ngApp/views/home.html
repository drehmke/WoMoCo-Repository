Balancing home and work?
It doesn't matter if you're married or a single mom; just entering the workforce or you're currently working, most companies don't support working mothers. It's up to you to find someone to take care of your children while you are at work. Even if it's just for a few hours between school and when you go home. There's daycare services, but you need to find a good one and sometimes, that takes time, trial and error.

By joining our community of working mothers, you can find and connect with others like yourself — get recommendations, share resources and build up the support network that YOU need.
