WoMoCo

Working Mothers Connections

In this fast-paced world, the professional mother has many hats to wear mom, provider, employee, leader, and teacher to name just a few. How do they keep it all together, how do they strike a balance with the demands of their professional and personal lives?

With the Working Mothers Connection (WoMoCo) the professional mother can connect with other professionals and share their experiences, give tips, network or just find out where they can get their hair done or a good mani-pedi!

The WoMoCo team, through personal experiences and polling professional women, have come to see that there is a need for a place where professional mother can come together, collaborate, or just find a good babysitter for that much-needed night out with their special someone.
